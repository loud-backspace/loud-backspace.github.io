Roommate Finder

Our roommate finder feature is the main event! Carefully catered to make finding you the best room as easy and stress-free as possible.

By filtering through ZIP code, area radius, gender, budget, and more, you can find exactly what you're looking for and chat with potential roommates once you've found a good match.

Create a Listing

Create a Listing

Rezidenc™ not only allows you to search for current listings, but also lets you create your own! Simply enter some basic information regarding the location and budget and you're ready to go.

Other users can search for and find your listing in the finder tab when their filters match the information you've entered in your listing. From there, you can chat and negotiate any further details.

Chat with Others

Chat with Others

Our built-in chat allows you to communicate with potential and current roommates without needing to share private personal information like phone numbers or emails.

View recent messages and the online status of users you've contacted through the main chat tab. Send images and videos to users through your device's gallery.

Set Preferences

Set Preferences

A key component of Rezidenc™ is setting your personal preferences. One of the most common ways roommates find each other is through mutual interests or needs.

We make it easy by allowing you to specify your own preferences, personal lifestyle, and needs on your profile. View or change your preferences at any time through your account page.
